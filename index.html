Unique Custom Furniture

We build beautiful pieces of furniture with Australian hardwood and epoxy resin. Each piece is custom made to our clients unique needs, no two pieces are alike. We are proudly based in the Macedon Ranges in Victoria and can ship our pieces Australia wide.

Australian Hardwood

We proudly use locally sourced, renewable Australian hardwood in our pieces: Jarrah, Messmate, Red Gum, Sassafras, Blackwood and more.

A Reason to Pause

Striking epoxy resin features cast against natural live edge timber creates a beautiful, eye catching piece that is guaranteed to grab your guests attention an give them a reason to pause.
